About The HER Thesis

An interactive exploration of seven remarkable women scientists whose contributions fundamentally changed our understanding of physics, chemistry, biology, and astronomy before 1950. Their stories deserve recognition in the annals of scientific history.

STEMposium 2025

Created for STEMposium 2025 at IISc Bangalore under the theme "History of Science (Mid-20th Century & Before)." This project highlights the hidden figures who shaped modern science through their groundbreaking pre-1950 discoveries.

Our Mission

To honor and illuminate the forgotten contributions of women in science before 1950, inspire future generations, and ensure their brilliant discoveries are never again relegated to footnotes in history.
